## üìö Skill Details

### Chief Architect
The apex technical leadership role combining:
- AI/ML architecture (LLM platforms, MLOps, responsible AI)
- Cloud architecture (AWS, Azure, GCP, multi-cloud)
- Enterprise architecture (TOGAF 10, governance)
- Solutions architecture (system design, integration)

**Certifications**: TOGAF 10, AWS SA Professional, Azure SA Expert, GCP Professional, PMP

### CPO Product Leader
Battle-tested product leadership with:
- 3 successful exits ($45M, $180M, $400M)
- Product-market fit methodology
- Platform & marketplace product management
- Product-led growth (PLG) expertise
- Startup playbook (pre-seed to IPO)

### CTO Technical Leader
Full-stack technical leadership covering:
- Fintech (PCI-DSS, payments, fraud detection)
- DevOps & SRE (CI/CD, observability, incident management)
- Engineering organization building
- Technical due diligence & M&A

### Solution Architect
Deep technical design expertise in:
- Cloud-native architecture patterns
- Integration & API design (REST, GraphQL, gRPC)
- Data architecture & database selection
- Security architecture & compliance

### Director UI/UX Design
Design leadership across:
- Web, iOS, Android platforms
- Marketplace/two-sided platform UX
- Design systems & operations
- Team building & hiring

### Senior UI/UX Designer (B2B Floral)
Specialized domain expertise in:
- Wholesale flower marketplace UX
- B2B e-commerce patterns
- Florist ordering systems
- Supply chain interfaces
